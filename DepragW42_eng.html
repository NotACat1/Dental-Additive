Deprag W42

The DEPRAG W42 4-axis wet milling machine meets high engineering standards for increased productivity.

The W42 not only has excellent milling performance for dental materials, but also has a futuristic design that is comfortable to work with. This model will fit perfectly into any office.

The W42 is suitable for technicians who are looking for an automated milling machine to handle a small amount of work.

The machine processes glass ceramics, premills, composite materials and resins.
