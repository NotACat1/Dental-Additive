Deprag W52

DEPRAG W52 is a 5-axis wet and dry milling machine capable of processing a wide range of materials.

The W52 comes standard with a powerful 62mm diameter spindle and is equipped with water cooling, which not only allows you to quickly and perfectly complete the processing of materials, but also ensures the stability and durability of the equipment for long-term work to the maximum extent.

The tool magazine includes up to 18 cutters from 0.4 to 3 mm. The milling machine automatically changes them and controls wear.

The kit comes with a cabinet, which integrates a filter module and a water tank, as well as 25 cutters of various shapes, diameters and coatings.

The machine processes Wax, PMMA, Composite Resins, Glass Ceramics, Titanium, Zircon and Cobalt-Chrome.
