Deprag W40

W40 is a 4-axis wet milling machine with a block holder installed. This model is designed for processing blocks of glass-ceramic composite materials.

The machine is equipped with a tool magazine for 4 cutters of different shapes and diameters, from 0.6 to 2.5 mm. The kit comes with a holder for processing blocks with 1 position. The remote monitoring system will allow you to remotely monitor the milling process.

The W40 milling machine is suitable for technicians who are going to go digital, while choosing the right digital equipment for beginners and not overpaying. You will receive a budget and reliable router from the premium manufacturer Deprag with optimal technical characteristics, accuracy and milling speed.

The set includes a cabinet with an integrated filter module and a water tank, as well as 25 cutters of various shapes, diameters and coatings.

The machine processes glass ceramics and resins.
