Deprag Gemstone 3D Pro-Multi

Gemstone SHT-Multi is a multilayer zirconia for general use. Due to its high strength of 1000 MPa, the material can be used for extended structures of more than three units. The translucency of 47% will allow you to use the SHT-Multi for full anatomical work.

SHT-Multi is available in 9 classic VITA tooth shades, making restoration easy and efficient for any lab.

  • Veneers

  • Tabs \ Overlays

  • Stump tab

  • Complete anatomy of the frontal zone

  • Complete anatomy of the masticatory group

  • Bridges up to 3 units

  • Bridges over 4 units

  • Tabs up to 3 units

  • Tabs more than 4 units

  • Full implant

  • Abutment
