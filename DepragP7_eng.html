Deprag P7

DEPRAG P7 is a modern kiln designed specifically for firing glass ceramics.

Kanthal wire is used as heating elements in P7 furnace, with high temperature resistance, fast temperature rise, long service life and stable resistance.

The device has a fast sintering function - up to 120 °C/min.

In addition, the P7 is also equipped with a number of smart features such as fast cooling control, PID temperature control system with ±1°C accuracy, and 200 preset programs that can be updated via Wi-Fi.

Basic equipment includes oil-free, maintenance-free vacuum pump with long life and low noise.
